Hi, I’m Ellen.

Ellen Perry grew up in a small town in southern Ohio, but has always dreamed big. She had planned on publishing her first novel by the time she turned eleven, but decided to wait until her brain was fully developed. As a child, she spent most of her free time with her nose in a book or scribbling on herself with markers. She left Ohio in 2013 to pursue illustration at Virginia Commonwealth University. While at school in Richmond, she discovered a love for tattoo art along the way, landing an apprenticeship at East End Dermagraphics and graduating from VCUarts in 2017 with a BFA in Communication Arts. She plans to have her tattoo license by the end of 2018.
